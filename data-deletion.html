Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: March 5, 2026

How to Request Data Deletion

If you want us to delete your data from AI Social Traveler, you can:

  1. Email us at warreng_t2@yahoo.co.uk with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include your Facebook Page name or Page ID in the email
  3. We will delete all stored data associated with your account within 30 days

What Data We Delete

  • Stored access tokens for your Facebook Page
  • Analytics and engagement data collected from your Page
  • Any content generation history associated with your account

What We Cannot Delete

Content already published to your Facebook Page belongs to your Page. You can delete published posts directly from Facebook.

Confirmation

After processing your request, we will send a confirmation email to the address you used to contact us.
