Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

AI Social Traveler ("we", "us", "our") operates as an AI-powered social media application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our app through Facebook Login, we may receive:

  • Your public Facebook profile information (name, profile picture)
  • Facebook Page data you grant access to (page name, page ID)
  • Comments and engagement data on posts made by our application

2. How We Use Information

  • To publish content (images, videos, Reels) to authorized Facebook Pages
  • To read and respond to comments on published posts
  • To gather engagement analytics for content optimization

3. Data Storage & Security

We store access tokens in encrypted form. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Data is stored on secure servers and retained only as long as necessary for the app to function.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Facebook/Meta Graph API to publish and manage content. Your use of Facebook is governed by Facebook's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time. See our Data Deletion page for instructions.
